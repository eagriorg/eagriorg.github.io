CUCURBITS :: MINOR :: PEST OF STINK BUG

 

 

10. Stink bug: Aspongopus janus (Pentatomidae: Hemiptera)
It is a large red and black bug found clinging to the leaves and tender shoots in large numbers. Nymphs and adults emit a buggy smell and suck sap from the tender parts and thereby devitalize the plants and retard their growth.
