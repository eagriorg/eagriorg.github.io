OKRA :: MAJOR :: PEST OF LEAF ROLLER

 

 

4. Leaf roller: Sylepta derogata (Pyraustidae: Lepidoptera)

 

Distribution and status: Throughout South East Asia. Minor pest
For host range, damage symptoms and bionomics refer cotton        

 

 

Management
Collect and destroy rolled leaves and spray carbaryl 50 WP 1.0 kg or phosalone 50 EC 1.0 L  in 500 L water/ha
