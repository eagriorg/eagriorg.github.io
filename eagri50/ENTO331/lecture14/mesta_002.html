Pests of Mesta

 

2. Root knot nematodes: Meloidogyne incognita

The root knot nematodes affect mesta plants which develop chlorosis. It is more pronounced in sandy loam soils. Proper crop rotations have been found to be useful.

1. Mealy bug: Maconellicoccus hirsutus (Pseudococcidae: Hemiptera)

Tip of the plant is covered and further growth of the plant is completely checked. Refer grapes for further information.
