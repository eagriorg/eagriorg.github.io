Pests of Citrus :: Minor Pests: :Thrips

12. Thrips: Thrips nilgiriensis (Thripidae: Thysanoptera)

Both nymphs and adults lacerate fruits causing ring like scarred area encircling the fruit stalk and irregular mottled patches on rind. Adult with yellowish, fringed wings.
