TUBER VEGETABLES ::MINOR PESTS:: BROWN LOOPER

 

 

 

Brown looper: Hyposidra successaria (Geometridae: Lepidoptera)
Larvae feed on leaves. Its egg, larval and pupal periods is 4-5, 18-29 and 8-9 days respectively.
