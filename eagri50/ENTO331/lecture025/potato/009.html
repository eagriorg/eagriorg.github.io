Trialeurodes vaporariorum (Aleyrodidae: Hemiptera)
            Both nymphs and adults desap the plants causing yellowing and wilting of plants. Adult small moth like insect lays pedicellate yellow eggs that turn dark chocolate brown on maturity. Nymph yellowish white.
Management: As given for Bemisia tabaci
