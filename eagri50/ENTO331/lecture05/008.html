PESTS OF PULSES :: Major Pests :: Lab-lab Leaf Miner

8. Lab-lab leaf miner: Cyphosticha coerulea (Gracillariidae: Lepidoptera)

 

Distribution and status: Throughout IndiaÂ 

 

Host plants: Blackgram, greengram, cowpea.

 

Damage symptoms: Tiny larvae bore into the epidermis of the leaf and forms blisters through mining.

 

Management: Spray the infested crop with endosulfan 35 EC 2.0 L or methyldemeton 750 ml in 700 litre water per ha.
