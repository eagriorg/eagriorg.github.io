TEA :: MAJOR PEST :: PURPLE MITE

 

 

Characters

Purple mite

(Calacarus carinatus)
 

Immature

stages		 Egg, larva, nymphs ( 2 instar)
Egg laying Under surface of matured leaves, more on Assam type
Egg period (days) 3 - 5
Nymphal period 3 - 6
Adult Very small, spindle shaped, dark purple, five longitudinal white waxy ridges on the dorsal side.
Symptoms of damage on leaves

Copper brown discoloration
