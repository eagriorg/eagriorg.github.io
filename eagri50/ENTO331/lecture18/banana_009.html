Pests of Banana :: Major Pests

 

9. Bagworm: Kophene cuprea (Psychidae: Lepidoptera)
        
Larva scrapes the chlorophyll and later it riddles with irregular holes on the leaf. Adult moth is brownish in colour. Brown larva covered in conical bag.
