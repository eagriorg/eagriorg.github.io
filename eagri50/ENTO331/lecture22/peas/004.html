PEAS :: MINOR PESTS :: AMERICAN BOLLWORM

 

1. American Bollworm: Helicoverpa armigera (Noctuidae: Lepidoptera)

 

Refer cotton

 

 

Management

 

Spray 5 L of chlorpyriphos 20EC or 2.0 kg of acephate 75 SP in 750 litres of water per ha.
