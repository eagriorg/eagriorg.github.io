BETELVINE :: MAJOR PEST :: MEALY BUG

 

 

4. Mealy bug : Geococcus citrinus (Pseudococcidae: Hemiptera)
Damage symptoms
Both nymph and adults found on the root regions and desap the root portions
Management
Spray chlorpyriphos 20 EC 2ml/L or dimethoate 2ml/L. Concentrate the spray towards the collar region.
