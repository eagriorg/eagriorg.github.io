PESTS OF WHEAT :: Major Pests :: Gram Pod Borer

4. Gram Pod Borer: Helicoverpa armigera (Noctuidae: Lepidoptera)


The gram pod borer attacks wheat at maturity. It feeds on the grains in the ear heads. The damage is more where wheat follows cotton.


Management: Spray 3 kg of carbaryl 50 WP or 2.0 L of quinalphos 25 EC in 500 L of water/ha.
