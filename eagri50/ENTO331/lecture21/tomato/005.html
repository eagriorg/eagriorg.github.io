TOMATO ::MAJOR PESTS::THRIPS

 

 

5. Thrips: T. tabaci, F. schultzi (Thripidae: Thysanoptera)
Damage symptoms


         Vector of tomato spotted wilt virus.  Lacerate leaf tissues and leaves become spotted and pale (Silvery streaks).  Feeds on flowers resulting in pre-mature dropping of flowers and also cause bud necrosis.
