PESTS OF RICE :: Minor Pests :: Earhead Stink Bug / Shield Bug / Red Spotted Bug

8. Earhead stink bug / Shield bug / Red spotted bug: Menida histrio  (Pentatomidae: Hemiptera)


Both nymphs and adults suck the ear heads and cause individual grains chaffy.
