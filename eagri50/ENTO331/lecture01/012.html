PESTS OF RICE :: Minor Pests :: Zigzag Striped Leafhopper

12. Zigzag striped leafhopper:  Recilia dorsalis (Cicadellidae: Hemiptera)

 Both nymphs and adults suck plant sap and cause tip drying and orange discoloration of both margins of leaves. Adults have white fore wings with pale brown bands forming the shape of W.
